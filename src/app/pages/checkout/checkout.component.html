SECTION 10 - WHAT DO WE DO WITH LANDOWNERS' AND MINERS' INFORMATION?

Root Diamonds does not only collect your information. It also collects information from the landowners and miners who pull our stones out of the ground.

Making our diamonds "storied" so that you can understand the lives of the artisanal miners at the beginning of diamond supply chains requires us to hold onto information about miners and landowners. This includes their image, information on the stones they found, biographic information, and their ambitions.

We are cautious to ensure that we only use this data the way we communicated to the miners and landowners when we met them. This means that we will share minimal data on our website and more comprehensive data only with the consumer that buys their diamond.

Returns Policy

We are sure you will be delighted with the loose-finished diamonds, raw diamonds, and rings you purchase from our online store. However, if you are dissatisfied with your product, we can offer 100% of your money back.

Returns are valid for all products within 30 days of receipt, and we will issue a refund within 14 days of the product arriving with us. Furthermore, if you buy a product from us and decide to change your mind before it has been shipped to you, we can instantly cancel your order.

If you decide to return your "Storied Diamond", please contact us contact@rootdiamonds.com, and we will guide you through the returns process. Your statutory rights are not impacted by making returns.

Please bear in mind that we will not be able to accept your return if your "Storied Diamond" is not in its original condition. For example, if a third-party jeweler has worked on your diamond or you have damaged it, your return will be refused.
